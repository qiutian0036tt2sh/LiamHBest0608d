/gmc - Sets gamemode in creative
/gms - Sets gamemode in survival
/gma - Sets gamemode in adventure
/gmsp Sets gamemode in spectator

/item [material] - Get 64 of any item
/item [material] [amount] - Get a specified amount of any item

/setchatdelay [off] - Removes the chat delay for unranked players
/setchatdelay [value] - Sets the chat delay for unranked players

/punish [IGN] - Open a punishment manager
/punish ban [IGN] - Open the punishment manager at the ban page
